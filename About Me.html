ABOUT ME

I'm a casual 20 years old full-time student in my last semester in pursuing Diploma in Computer Science. My passion in translating videos from Korean to English comes from my hobby which is watching South Korea's shows and exploring it's culture that I fell in love it. The other main reason I started contributing to the community is because in my life I've met a lot of good people that helped me in a lot of way and I figured out, the only way for me as a student without any income to give back to others is by my decent understanding in the South Korean language and culture that is popular worldwide.

WHAT I DO

As said above, I voluntarily translate videos on South Korean live video streaming service platform, V LIVE; that is used by celebrities to communicate with their fans. I am currently in two teams, one for popular rookie group "ITZY" and "fromis_9".

I also time the videos which is a crucial part that people easy to overlook. A viewer can't understand properly if it's out of order. And I consider myself trustworthy as I am very sensitive about precise timing.
