ABOUT ME

A casual 21 years old full-time student in my last semester in pursuing Diploma in Computer Science. My passion in translating videos from Korean to English comes from my hobby of watching South Korea's shows and realizing that understanding something, goes a long way. It leads to a good communication and the word "공감" which means sympathy. The other main reason I started contributing to the community is because I wanted to give back whatever help I received from others in my life and I figured one of the ways for me as a student without any income to do so is by utilizing my decent understanding of the South Korean language and its culture that is popular worldwide in this present time.

WHAT I DO

As said above, I voluntarily translate videos on South Korean live video streaming service platform, V LIVE; that is used by celebrities to communicate with their fans. I am currently in two teams, one for popular rookie group "ITZY" and "fromis_9".

I also time the videos which is a crucial part that people easy to overlook, because, obviously viewers will have a hard time trying to figure out where does that certain line of translation belongs to if it's out of order, right?
